Research Interests

My primary interests lie in the intersection between algebraic geometry and geometric representation theory. I am also interested in mathematical physics, especially the applications and implications of Supersymmetric Quantum Field Theory to Geometric Langlands as well as other phenonenoms in mathematics. I am also interested in Homotopy Type Theory, as well as computer verifications of mathematics theorem. Lately I have been thinking about knot homologies and different ways to think about them.
