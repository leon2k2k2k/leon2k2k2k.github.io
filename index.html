Research Interests

My primary research interests are mathematical physics, namely condensed matter physics and topological field theories. Besides math, I am also interested in physics and CS (DevOps, type theory and programming language).

Paper and Preprints

  • Abelian Duality in Topological Field Theories: Preprint following Freed-Teleman. For an extended version, see my senior thesis. Any comment is appreciated!
    • We prove a topological version of abelian duality where the gauge groups are finite abelian. The theories are finite homotopy TFTs, topological analogues of the $p$-form $U(1)$ gauge theories where the gauge group is finite abelian. Using Brown-Comenetz duality, we extend our results to $\pi$-finite spectra.
