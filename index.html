Research Interests

My primary interests lie in the intersection between mathematical physics and geometric representation theory. Another interests of mine is homotopy theory and its applications, for example, Homotopy type theory.

Lately I have been thinking about different approaches to knot homologies. In the fall of 2020, I am completing my honor thesis (see below) on abelian duality in topological field theory with Professor Dan Freed .

Paper and Preprints

  • Abelian Duality in Topological Field Theories: I proof a version of abelian duality as equivalences of TFTs (up to tensoring with an invertible TFT), following Freed-Teleman. Any comment is appreciated!
