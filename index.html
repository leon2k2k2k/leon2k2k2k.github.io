Research Interests

My primary interests lie in the intersection between algebraic geometry and geometric representation theory. I am also interested in mathematical physics, especially the applications and implications of Supersymmetric Quantum Field Theory to Geometric Langlands. I am also interested in Homotopy Type Theory, as well as computer verifications of mathematics theorem.

Lately I have been thinking about different approaches to knot homologies. In the fall of 2020, I will be completing my honor thesis over Chern-Simons theory under Professor Dan Freed.
