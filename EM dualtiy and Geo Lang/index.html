Electric-Magnetic Duality and Physics of Geometric Langlands

Learning Seminar, Fall 2020

Overview:

This is a learning seminar about EM duality and the physics of Geometric Langlands. The current plan is to understand electric-magnetic duality in 4D N=4 Yang Mills. From there, there are a lot of possible directions to take...
